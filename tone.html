Tone

Engaging but not pushy

Yes: Updating your profile will increase your scholarship matches.

No: Update your profile now to get more matches today!

Professional but not corporate

Yes: Your application has been accepted. Check your email for more details.

No: Your scholarship application has been accepted. You may now refer to your email inbox for further instructions on how to proceed.

Friendly but not fun

Yes: Thanks. Your profile will be ready in just a few minutes.

No: Sweet! Your profile'll be rockin' in a few!

Informative but not overwhelming

Yes: I agree to the terms & conditions.

No: By checking this box you are agreeing to the terms & conditions created solely for this product. You are responsible for the information you provide to us.

Use this tone to guide the visual and verbal communication style. Tone is especially helpful when crafting system messages like errors and warnings.
